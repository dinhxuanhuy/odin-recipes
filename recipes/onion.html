onion

Description

ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of beef broth
  • 1/2 cup of grated cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Peel and slice the onion into thin rings.
  2. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the sliced onions and cook until they are soft and caramelized, about 15-20 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add the beef broth and bring to a simmer.
  6. If desired, top with grated cheese and let it melt before serving.

Enjoy your meal!
